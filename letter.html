Pac-Man

Pac-Man is SRM MUNSoc's first-ever lateral thinking game show. Inspired by the popular BBC TV show Only Connect, participants race against time to guess a common thread connecting 4 seemingly unrelated hints. Participants can enter as individuals or form teams of up to 4 people, and engage in an exciting quiz-based adaptation of a much-loved trivia game format.

Pentagon

Pentagon is a multi-disciplinary competition to honour the mark the old world has left on us. We want to see the world through the participants' eyes, be it art, pictures, or words.

Siddharth Sanjay

Secretary-General, SRM MUN Society 2021-22

“We must be the willow, not the oak, in the lowering storm.”

We are an organization that takes immense pride in our 11-year legacy of MUNs, to us it's not merely about the distinction of being South Asia's largest conference that takes precedence but rather the experience and quality of our endeavours. Through the uncertainties faced by all of us during the pandemic, we adapted and challenged the legacy, transitioning into SRM MUN Society. This change reflects our capability as an organization to expand beyond just MUNs. The beauty of our organization is that we take in members with diverse interests and give them an opportunity to learn and become a part of something larger than themselves and thus be a better version of themselves, creating a sense of achievement and belonging like nowhere else. SRM MUN Society is a community where, much like how heated debates in MUNs lead to a resolution, collaborative efforts and passionate discussions lead to creative solutions. It is hence my absolute pleasure to invite all of you to our events where you will get to witness what can be created by focused, talented and exceptional individuals and experience for yourselves the best that college events have to offer.
