Pac-Man

Pac-Man is SRM MUNSoc's first-ever lateral thinking game show. Inspired by the popular BBC TV show Only Connect, participants race against time to guess a common thread connecting 4 seemingly unrelated hints. Participants can enter as individuals or form teams of up to 4 people, and engage in an exciting quiz-based adaptation of a much-loved trivia game format.

Pentagon

Pentagon is a multi-disciplinary competition to honour the mark the old world has left on us. We want to see the world through the participants' eyes, be it art, pictures, or words.
