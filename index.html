Pac-Man

Pac-Man is SRM MUNSoc's first-ever lateral thinking game show. Inspired by the popular BBC TV show Only Connect, participants race against time to guess a common thread connecting 4 seemingly unrelated hints. Participants can enter as individuals or form teams of up to 4 people, and engage in an exciting quiz-based adaptation of a much-loved trivia game format.

Pentagon

Pentagon is a multi-disciplinary competition to honour the mark the old world has left on us. We want to see the world through the participants' eyes, be it art, pictures, or words.

SRMMUN

About Us

At the onset of this new decade, SRMMUN transformed into the SRMMUN Society in order to expand its horizons and encompass a larger spectrum of participants across a wide range of interests. SRM MUN Society comprises of two parallel branches - the Organising Committee and the Delegation:

  • The Organising Committee is responsible for facilitating the working of all activities carried out within the organisation. It is further categorised into six specialised domains that work in tandem to organise events and activities throughout the year.
  • The Delegation serves as the lynchpin of SRM MUN Society and takes part primarily in MUN conferences across the country. This year, it has evolved into an epicentre of creativity that encourages and trains students in public speaking, debate, art, writing, quizzes, journalism, and photojournalism, in addition to Model UN training.
